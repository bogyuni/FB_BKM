새해맞이 <굿(GOOD)이로구나!> New Year Concert <It is GOOD!>

Time
January 7, 14, 28, Every Saturday 15:00
Venue
Yeonak-dang Hall
Ticket Price
A seat 10,000won, B seat 8,000won
Cast/Direct
Association for Gudeok folk arts preservation, Association for Suyeong historical folk arts preservation, Association for Busan folk arts preservation
Audience Age
Children in elementary school or older
Reservation

Another seasonal custom in Busan, it is GOOD!

The stage is filled with folk performances transmitted by the Association of Busan folk art preservation. The performers create new seasonal custom with the people in Busan. The performance is not only joyful and enthusiastic, but also full of hope greeting lucky new year.

Program

1. 7.(Sat) Dongnae ground stepping, Dongnae drum dance, Dongnae hallyang dance 1. 14.(Sat) Guddeokmangkkae work song, Busan Gobundori praying event, Busan Farmer’s music 1. 28.(Sat) Suyeong ground stepping, Jwasuyeongeobang village festival, Suyeong Yaryu, Suyeong Nongcheong Farmer’s gathering
